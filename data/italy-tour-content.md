# Italy Tour 2026 - Tour Details

## Introduction

Hi Nipun,

I hope you're doing well.

I wanted to reach out following our recent matches between Brisbane City and Moreton City U14 Girls.

Through my private academy, SportsLab Academy, I am currently organising an Italy Tour in December 2026 and I'm looking to finalise a strong and competitive squad capable of competing against some of the top academy environments in Italy. The tour is independent from Brisbane City FC and is not affiliated with any club. The aim is simply to provide a unique development opportunity for talented players from across South East Queensland.

## Tour Itinerary

The current itinerary includes:

- 3 academy training sessions with Como 1907
- 3 friendly matches against professional academy teams
- Potential fixtures against clubs such as Como, Fiorentina, Milan and Inter (subject to final confirmation)
- Attendance at a Serie A match
- Cultural and team-building activities throughout the trip
- 9 nights accommodation with breakfast and dinner included

## Pricing

The estimated cost is approximately **AUD $2,750 per person** (excluding flights), with a deposit required only once we begin confirming numbers and bookings.

## Eligibility

If you have suitable players in your **U13, U14 or U15 age groups**, I would love for them to be considered as well.

## Interested Parties

I was wondering whether you might be willing to either:

1. Share this opportunity with families you feel may be interested, or
2. Put me in touch with players and families who you believe would benefit from and be capable of competing in this environment.

I'm certainly not looking to recruit players or take anyone away from their club. My only objective is to assemble the strongest possible squad for this specific tour and give motivated players the chance to experience a high-level football environment overseas.

## Next Steps

I'd also be more than happy to organise a short meeting with yourself and any interested families this week or next week to explain the tour in more detail and answer any questions.

I'll attach the tour presentation for reference.

Thank you for your time, and please let me know if you think there may be some interest from your players and families.
